How this works

How you're graded

Your score has three parts, and they multiply:

  • Mastery — did you actually learn the cards?
  • Engagement — did you study across many days, not just one?
  • Completion — did you work through the whole deck, not just the easy cards?

You can't max out by cramming, and you can't max out by clicking through quickly. Steady study wins.
