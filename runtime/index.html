How you're graded

Your score combines two things:

  • Mastery — did you actually learn the cards, across the whole deck?
  • Engagement — did you study across many days, not just one?

Mastery counts every card in the deck, so you can't max out on just the easy ones — and you can't max out by cramming or clicking through quickly. Steady study wins.
